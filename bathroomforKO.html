    • Organization Tips for Kid's Bathrooms

    By putting a towel hanger on the wall, and writing the child's name on the hanger, there will never be a problem with wet towels on the floor.



    Make having fun in the tub a more organized and fun time! Separate baskets, so your children can only take one basket out at a time, and there will be no complaining when it comes time to clean up!



    When it comes to brushing your teeth, and washing your face, a great way to keep tooth brushes, and wash clothes separated is a small container, decorated for each child differently so they know which container is theirs.



    Rushing to get ready in the morning can be supper stressful, but by organizing all hair and jewelry accessories into cute containers that avoids tangeling, will make the morning run much smoother!



    By hanging a small shelf in unreachable spots for children, it keeps mom and dads special hair products out of reach for little fingers to get in to.
